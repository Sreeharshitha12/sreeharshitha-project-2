Character Bios

Iron Man (Tony Stark)

The genius billionaire playboy philanthropist who becomes the armored superhero, Iron Man.

Interesting Fact: Tony Stark's AI assistant, J.A.R.V.I.S., is named after his father's butler.

Captain America (Steve Rogers)

The super-soldier who fights for freedom with his indestructible shield.

Interesting Fact: Captain America's shield is made from vibranium, the same material found in Wakanda.
